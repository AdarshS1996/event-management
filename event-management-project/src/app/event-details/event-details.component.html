About the Events
The Federation presents “The International Public Speaking Summit 2021: The celebration of public speaking”. IPSS 2021 is the brainchild of the organization which will stand for ingenuity, excellence and redefine the standards of debate competitions. It will be an amalgamation of fierce discussion and intellectual projection, that will contribute to knowledge expansion amongst the youth.

IPSS 2021 Aims to:
  • Review – A review of the structure of current public speaking events conducted.
  • Resolve – A resolve to provide participants with a memorable debating experience.
  • Reform – A reform of various public speaking events to resemble the standard of competitions conducted internationally.
Events: 10 competitions are set to be conducted on different dates, which are the Youth Parliament, Modul United Nations, Public Forum Debate, Just a Minute, Speaking to Persuade, Speaking to Inform, 1 vs. 1 Style of Debating, Policy Debating, Block and tackle, and Open Category.

Dates: 8th January 2021 to 12th January 2021

Venue: Online – Zoom

Eligibility Criteria:
  • School students – 8th Grade onwards
  • University/College students
  • Open Category for Corporate employees
