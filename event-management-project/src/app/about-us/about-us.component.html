About the Organisation
The Federation is a one-of-a-kind organization based in India, founded in June 2016 by Mr. Varun Jain. Our organisational setup comprises of young visionaries who see potential in the future of public speaking and seek to revolutionize the way they are organized within the nation. Through our collaborations with various premier institutions, we ensure that these institutions which conduct various public speaking events at their level meet the standards of the ones conducted at the global level. Our team equips the institution, the participating students, and other staff members with the latest criteria, structures and other essential requisites needed for a successful public speaking summit. The knowledge acquired consequently is spread to other organisations such that Indian public speaking competitions become a worthy medium of learning.
We work to promote international connectivity by entering into collaborations with institutions organizing Model United Nations (MUNs) at a global level. Since our inception, we have established operations in several Indian states as well as other countries and expanded to accommodate a variety of departments such as MUN Conferences, Youth Parliament, International Press and Conventional debating.
Our vision is to innovate in terms of various styles of public speaking and to create knowledge.
